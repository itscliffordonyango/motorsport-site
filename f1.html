History of Formula 1

Formula 1, the highest class of open-wheeled auto racing defined by the Fédération Internationale de l'Automobile (FIA), began in 1950. The first world championship was won by Italian Giuseppe Farina at Silverstone.

Today, F1 features 10 teams and 20 drivers competing in 24 Grands Prix worldwide, pushing the limits of speed, technology, and human performance.
